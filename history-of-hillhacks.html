Hillhacks began in 2013 as a conversation between Ritu and Tenzin, founders of the Dharamsala International Film Festival, and Akiba, a hacker/maker from Freaklabs in Japan. It soon expanded to include Ayush Gai from Ghoomakhad (Infinity Hackbase) in nearby Rakkar, and sva from Germany. Within a year, the first hillhacks took place.
