hillhacks is entirely volunteer-run and participatory. Everyone who attends helps out on-site with daily tasks like cleaning and communications. Offer your get-it-done attitude, bright ideas, and clever hands.

If you would like to help before or after the event, anything from accounting to organising, we welcome your participation.

The best way to get started is to join the hillhacks mailing list and discover what needs to be done now.

We also welcome donors and sponsors to provide materials and to lower costs for our participants. Please see our Partners page in the wiki for more details
