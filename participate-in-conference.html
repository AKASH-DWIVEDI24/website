Dates: 18-21 May 2017

hillhacks is an annual event for free-thinkers, tinkerers and creative minds, artists, techies and hobbyists, hackers and makers, crafters and scientist, humanists and engineers, activists and coders, geeks and nerds, designers and philosophers, natural and artificial intelligences and other interesting beings doing interesting stuff.

For our next event in May 2017, we invite you to share, demonstrate, teach and talk about the things you love. We are open to all topics but especially solicit proposals on the following:

  • Hardware
  • Software
  • Data
  • Security
  • Futurism
  • Freedom
  • Art
  • Architecture
  • Ecology
  • Nature
