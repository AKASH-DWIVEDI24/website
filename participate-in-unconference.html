May 15-17 & May 22-31

The Unconference portion of hillhacks (before and after the conference) is chock full of activities and projects. Jump in and play!

No CFP is needed to present a talk, workshop, or activity during the unconference. Find a space, gather an audience, and conduct your session. There will be facilities for ad hoc scheduling on-site.

Previous years have seen unconference activities including:

  • Meditation & yoga
  • Furniture building
  • Nature walks & trekking
  • Hair cutting and coloring
  • Radio station set-up
  • International cooking
  • Collaborative artworks
  • Unicycle
  • Music and dance jams
  • Set-up and clean-up
