Dates: 15-17 May 2017 and 22-31 May 2017

The unconference portion of hillhacks (before and after the main conference) is chock full of activities and projects. Jump in and play!

No formal proposal is needed to present a talk, workshop, or activity during the unconference. Find a space, gather an audience and conduct your session. There will be facilities for ad hoc scheduling on-site.

Previous years have seen unconference activities including:

  • Meditation & yoga
  • Furniture building
  • Nature walks & trekking
  • Hair cutting and coloring
  • Radio station set-up
  • International cooking
  • Collaborative artworks
  • Unicycle
  • Music and dance jams
  • Set-up and clean-up
